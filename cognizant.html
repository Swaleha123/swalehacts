About Cognizant Company

Photo of company:
Cognizant

 

Cognizant is an American multinational corporation that provides IT services, including digital, technology, consulting, and operations services. It is headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, United States. Cognizant is part of the NASDAQ-100 and trades under CTSH.It was founded on 26th january,1994.The founders are Kumar Mahadeva and Francisco D'souza.The CEO of the company is Brian Humphires.
